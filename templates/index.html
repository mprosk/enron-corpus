How to Use

Quick Search

Enter any text to search across all email fields (subject, sender, recipient, and body content). This is the fastest way to find emails.

Advanced Search

Click "Advanced Search" to expand additional filters. You can search specific fields:

  • From: Search by sender email or name
  • To: Search by recipient email or name
  • Participant: Find emails where someone appears as either sender OR recipient
  • Subject: Search within email subject lines
  • Body: Search within email body content
  • Path: Filter by folder path (e.g., "lay-k/sent" or "inbox")
  • Date Range: Filter emails by date (most emails are from 1999-2003)

You can combine multiple fields to narrow your search.

Tips

  • All searches are case-insensitive
  • Results are limited to the first 1,000 matches per search
  • Click any email in the results to view its full contents
  • Use the random email buttons to explore the corpus
